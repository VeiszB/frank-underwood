Biography

Francis J. Underwood was born on November 5, 1959 in Gaffney, South Carolina.

In 1984, at age 25, Underwood was elected to his first public office, being the South Carolina State Senate. He won re-election in 1988, and in 1990, he sought the office of South Carolina’s 5th Congressional District.

Underwood won the 1990 House Election, and served for another 22 years. In 2005, he became the House Majority Whip.
